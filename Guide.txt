DIRECTION:
Control your paddle to hit the ball back and forth.
There are 2 modes of gameplay: 1 Player and 2 Players.
The goal of 1 Player mode is to reach highest score by making the ball stay in the course.
The goal of 2 Players mode is to reach the winning-score before the opponent does.

GUIDE:
*1 Player mode:
Use your arrow keys to move your paddle in the down-side of the course.

*2 Players mode:
-Player 1: Use W,A,S,D keys to move your paddle in the up-side of the course.
-Player 2: Use arrow keys to move your paddle in the down-side of the course.
